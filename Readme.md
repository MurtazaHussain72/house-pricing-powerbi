---

###  Regional & Sales Performance Analysis

This section evaluates regional contribution and pricing behavior.

**Key Findings:**
- Zealand and Jutland dominate total sales volume.
- Price per square meter varies significantly across regions, with Zealand leading.
- Property age strongly influences purchase price.
- Demographic and population patterns affect pricing tiers.

**Business Value:**
- Supports region-based investment prioritization.
- Improves pricing strategy alignment.
- Assists in expansion and portfolio diversification planning.

---

###  House Type Performance & Market Drivers

This section analyzes pricing efficiency and return potential across property types.

**Comparisons Include:**
- Offer vs Purchase Price by House Type
- Average Square Meter & SQM Price
- Inflation, Interest, and Yield metrics

**Key Observations:**
- Apartments command the highest price per square meter.
- Larger properties (e.g., Farms) show higher total value but lower SQM efficiency.
- Yield differences suggest varying return profiles across property types.

**Business Value:**
- Helps optimize property investment portfolios.
- Aligns pricing models with return expectations.
- Assesses macroeconomic influence on housing performance.
